## About

I started my professional path in **graphic design**, creating visual resources for websites, blogs and online communities. Design has always shaped the way I think about digital products and interfaces.

Later I worked for several years in the **hospitality industry**, eventually becoming a **Sous Chef**. Working in professional kitchens taught me discipline, teamwork and how to perform under pressure — skills that translate surprisingly well into building and shipping things.

A couple of years ago I started transitioning into **web development**, combining my design background with modern frontend tools to build interfaces and digital experiences.

Today I focus on **frontend development**, building projects with **JavaScript and React**, while also exploring how **AI-assisted development workflows** can accelerate design and implementation.
